The Programme

What students
actually learn.

The camp runs across two tracks — applications and careers — delivered over 5–7 days. NTU faculty anchor the AI literacy modules; Comet Guides run the application workshops.

🤖

AI Literacy for Students

How large language models work, what they're good at, and where they fall short — taught by NTU faculty with hands-on sessions in NTU's labs.

✍️

AI-Assisted Application Writing

Using AI tools to brainstorm, structure, and refine personal statements and supplemental essays — with Comet Guides reviewing every draft in real time.

🔍

University Research with AI

How to use AI to map fit, compare programmes, and build a balanced school list — moving faster and with more rigour than a spreadsheet allows.

💼

Career Pathways & AI

Which careers AI is reshaping, which skills will matter, and how to frame early choices — internships, majors, extracurriculars — around what's coming.

🎤

Interview Prep with AI Simulation

Students run simulated university interviews with AI, receive structured feedback, and practise with Comet Guides — covering Oxford-style, Oxbridge, and panel formats.

📊

Build Your AI Portfolio

Every student leaves with a short AI project — a career research report, a redlined essay draft, or a mapped school list — that can go straight into their application materials.
